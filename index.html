About Me

I have recently moved Leibniz University Hannover, joining the Institute for Algebraic Geometry as a PostDoc, working under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Klaus Hulek. Before that I was a Research Associate at the Department of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Bath since 2013, supervised by Dr. Alastair Craw. I was an SFB-TR/45 PostDoc in Bonn and Mainz from 2009 to 2013, supervised by Prof. Dr. Daniel Huybrechts and Prof. Dr. Manfred Lehn. I graduated with PhD in Mathematics from Stanford University in 2009, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Jun Li.

Research

My research focuses on several aspects in algebraic geometry, including the geometry and topology of moduli spaces of sheaves and complexes on K3 surfaces and some Calabi-Yau threefolds, holomorphic symplectic manifolds, derived categories and stability conditions.
